BMI Calculator

Calculate your Body Mass Index and understand your health category

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight. The formula is BMI = kg/m² where kg is a person's weight in kilograms and m² is their height in meters squared. Use this calculator to determine your BMI and see where you fall in the healthy weight range.
